WHAT IS QUANT CLUB?

Quant Club, IIT KGP is a student body that aims at increasing the awareness of Quantitative Finance as a viable career option amongst the student community at IIT Kharagpur. Investopedia describes quants as the Rocket Scientists of Wall Street. We describe ourselves as a group of quant enthusiasts. The nature of work we do requires a unique blend of mathematics, finance and computer skills. In simple words, Quantitative Analysts a.k.a Quants design and implement complex models that allow financial firms to price and trade securities.
